About Me

I'm Htet Wai Yan, a Robotic & Software Engineer based in Yangon, Myanmar. I'm currently studying Computer Science at the University of the People on a full scholarship.

I was awarded the Best Coach Award at the World Robot Olympiad Myanmar 2025, where I guided my students to the Singapore International Round. I run both free and paid robotic classes to empower Myanmar's next generation of engineers.

Currently preparing for Robotic Engineering at KMITL, and co-founding RoboPath Myanmar to build a lasting tech education ecosystem.
